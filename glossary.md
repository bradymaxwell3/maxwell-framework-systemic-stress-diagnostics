# Glossary

Energy Slack — Surplus capacity in the energy system relative to demand and volatility.  
Fiscal Slack — Government maneuvering room across spending, revenue, and debt‑service constraints.  
NPDI — Net Private Domestic Investment; measures private‑sector capital formation.  
Rhizome Damping — Propagation‑control mechanism limiting shock transmission across domains.  
Regime‑Shift — Transition between stability phases triggered by multi‑signal convergence.  
Inflection Point Detector (IPD) — Algorithm identifying early signs of regime transition.  
Live Stress Tape (LST) — Rolling, integrated stress measure across all domains.  
Risk Envelopes — Multi‑horizon contextual bands for interpreting stress levels.  
Propagation Engine — Core logic that integrates inputs and applies damping.  
Phase‑Transition Marker — Signal indicating a potential shift in system stability.
